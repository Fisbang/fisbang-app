Turn Green Idea into Reality

Fisbang is abbreviation of FISika (physic)and BANGunan (building) in Indonesian language. We use theory and practical experience in building physics for contributing in green building. For us, building physics has three mayor subject : thermal, lighting and accoustic. One of our thought is how to work integrited building physics with economical factor such us return of investment

But building is not about your home, office or hotel. By spirit of thinking the best for people, we want to drive fisbang into broader area later.
